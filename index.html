About Me

I started working with website development and design in spring 2017. I registered for the Website Development and Design certificate program at Portland Community College in Oregon where I maintained a 4.0 throughout the program and became a memeber of Phi Theta Kappa, the Alpha Eta Iota Chapter. In addition, I hold a previous Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology from Georgia College and State University, 2008. I enjoy learning new concepts, working with a team, and contributing and helping others.

Currently, I am living in the Atlanta metro area where I enjoy hiking, camping, and exploring new places to eat with my husband and two dogs.
