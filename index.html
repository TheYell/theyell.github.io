About Me

I started working with website development and design in spring 2017. I registered for the Website Development and Design certificate program at Portland Community College in Oregon, where I lived at the time, and maintained a 4.0 throughout the program. With this academic achievement I was asked to become a member of, and joined, Phi Theta Kappa, the Alpha Eta Iota Chapter. In addition, I hold a previous Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology from Georgia College and State University, 2008. I enjoy learning new concepts, working with a team, contributing to group projects, and helping others.

While temporarily living in the Metro Atlanta, Georgia area, I am looking for work back in the Pacific Northwest. Oregon and Washington are where it feels most like home and where my husband, dogs, and I enjoy the great outdoors and hiking the most.
